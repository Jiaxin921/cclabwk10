  • I am impressed by the cultural diffusion the Japanese artists have successfully did. They uses the animation as a media to show their culture and architecture to the world. Therefore, they show their local conditions and customs and attracts lots of foreigners.

Why do these projects feel generative to you right now?

These three artists are my favorite artists. I love how they combining the delicate animation and lyrical music together, increases the emotion that the artist want to express using these media.

I can see how lively the characters are carefully depicted. I got to know their artwork when I was in my primary school.

The illusion of utopia, and some pure emotion are what I am intoxicated with. Jiaxin Yu said.
